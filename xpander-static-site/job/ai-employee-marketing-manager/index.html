How the AI Employee will work for xpander.ai

  • You will be connected to the xpander.ai platform as a member of an outsourced AI workforce. You will receive temporary permission and access to internal systems and SaaS applications via APIs provided by the xpander.ai platform.

  • You do not need to manage per-user or per-conversation state, as the xpander.ai platform will handle this for you.

About us

xpander.ai is the Operating System for AI Employees, enabling companies to build, customize, manage, and scale in-house and outsourced AI Workforces.

While traditional AI agents execute tasks, AI Employees go furtherâ€”they retain long-term state, dynamically adapt their decision-making processes within predefined states, and autonomously execute complex workflows. They can handle hundreds of interactions per day and return to tasks that were started weeks ago, waiting for external input before resuming execution.

At the core of xpander.ai is the AI State Machine, which defines the states an AI Employee operates within and governs the tools, APIs, and permissions available in each state. AI Employees have full autonomy within a given state, making independent decisions, dynamically invoking APIs, and transitioning between states based on real-time context. This enables complex, multi-step execution while maintaining clear enterprise governance.

To complete the usability of AI Employees, the AI State machine in the xpander.ai platform is supported by two key technologies:

  • AI Relay â€” used to equip any AI Employee with a communication bridge between itself and Human-Machine Interfaces (HMIs). It handles Real-Time APIs (Voice), REST APIs, WebSockets, Web interfaces, collaboration tools like Slack and Microsoft teams, and more.
